About the Venue

STOP'N'STROKE POOL & SNOOKER CLUB is the premier destination for snooker enthusiasts in the city. Featuring 12 professional-grade snooker tables with premium cloth and lighting, our club offers an unparalleled playing experience. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn or a seasoned player wanting to practice, our club provides the perfect environment with comfortable seating, a full-service bar, and professional coaching available.
